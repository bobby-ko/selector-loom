Description

Depend on nonstick bakeware designed to deliver baked goodness with the sturdy, reliable Circulon Nonstick Bakeware 9-Inch x 13-Inch Rectangular Cake Pan.

Superior nonstick technology and solution-driven design set Circulon bakeware apart from other nonstick bakeware. The technology begins with raised circles on the cake pan surface that boast premium nonstick. The rolled-rim design includes distinctive chocolate brown color inside and out, and wide handles for plenty of grip, even with bulky oven mitts. Oven safe to 450°F, this bakeware brings the Circulon kitchen-time-and-energy-saving approach to baking home. And with a Hassle-Free Lifetime Guarantee, the cake pan truly delivers for the well-rounded life.

Take the cake pan, one designed for delicious, dependable nonstick baking every time, with the efficient, stylish Circulon Nonstick Bakeware 9-Inch x 13-Inch Rectangular Cake Pan.
