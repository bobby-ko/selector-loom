Description

The Dyson Airwrap Multi Styler Complete Long harnesses an aerodynamic phenomenon called the Coanda effect, which is powered by the Dyson digital V9 motor. It uses air to attract and wrap hair to the barrel, or surface of the brush, with no extreme heat required.


Curl. Shape. Smooth and hide flyaways.

Coanda to Curl

Create clockwise and anticlockwise curls with one barrel. Enhanced Coanda airflow attracts and self-wraps hair in both directions for voluminous curls or waves. By combining powerful airflow and controlled heat, the Dyson Airwrap™ multi-styler can style and dry at the same time. With no extreme heat.

Intelligent Technology

The 13-blade impeller spins at up to 110,000rpm, generating 3.2kPa. Powerful enough to produce the air pressure that creates the Coanda effect. Moreover, the Intelligent heat control measures airflow temperature over 40 times a second to keep temperature at an optimum level.

Organised and Sophisticated

The kit comes with a storage case cushioned with soft fabric to keep your Dyson Airwrap multi styler and its attachments neatly organised. The case includes a removable lid that also works as a non-slip mat for the styler.
